About Deya

Deya is a ceramic artist based in Oakland, California. Her work draws inspiration from natural textures, childhood memories, and everyday rituals. Each piece is hand-thrown, hand-glazed, and made with love in her home studio.

She creates functional pottery fired at Cone 5 and Cone 10, balancing everyday utility with expressive design. Her small-batch process ensures that no two pieces are ever exactly alike.
